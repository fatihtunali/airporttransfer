1. **Email 1:** Introduction + Value Proposition
2. **Email 2:** Follow-up with social proof (if no reply in 5 days)
3. **Email 3:** Final attempt with urgency (if no reply in 10 days)

## OUTREACH SCHEDULE

### Recommended Daily Volume
- **Week 1-2:** 20 emails/day (to warm up email domain)
- **Week 3+:** 50 emails/day

### Weekly Schedule
- **Monday:** Research + find new contacts
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** Send Email 1 to new contacts
- **Friday:** Send follow-ups (Email 2, Email 3)

### Response Handling
- **Interested:** Send registration link + offer call
- **Questions:** Answer promptly, address concerns
- **Not interested:** Thank them, move on
- **No response after 3 emails:** Archive, try again in 3 months
