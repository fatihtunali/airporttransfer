# Supplier Outreach Campaign - 100 Emerging Market Cities

## Target Selection Criteria

Cities selected based on:
1. **High tourism volume** - International arrivals
2. **Fragmented supply** - No dominant platform
3. **Growing markets** - Tourism increasing YoY
4. **Cash/pay-later culture** - Fits our model
5. **Language accessibility** - English or Turkish-speaking suppliers
